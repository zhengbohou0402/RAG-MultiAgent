SmartCloud Billing, Orders, Renewal, Refund, and Invoice Policy

Document type: official billing policy
Source type: official
Trust label: SmartCloud official material

Billing concepts
- Order: the purchase or renewal transaction for a cloud product.
- Invoice: the tax document generated after payment is completed.
- Unpaid amount: the total amount waiting for payment in the current tenant.
- Monthly spend: the accumulated spending of the tenant in the current billing cycle.

Renewal rules
- Subscription ECS, database, and GPU products can be renewed manually or automatically.
- Auto-renewal should be enabled at least 3 days before expiration to avoid service interruption.
- If auto-renewal fails because of insufficient balance, SmartCloud creates a billing alert and a support case.

Refund rules
- Unused subscription resources can request a proportional refund when no abuse or policy violation exists.
- Pay-as-you-go resources are billed by usage and usually cannot be refunded after usage is generated.
- Refund review SLA is 1 business day for standard customers and 4 business hours for enterprise customers.

Invoice workflow
1. Open Billing Center.
2. Select paid orders.
3. Confirm invoice title and tax number.
4. Choose electronic invoice or paper invoice.
5. Submit the request and wait for finance review.

Finance Order Agent behavior
- When the user asks for bills, invoices, renewal, refund, unpaid amount, or cost optimization, route to Finance Order Agent.
- The agent should query tenant-level billing data from MySQL when available.
- The answer should include current unpaid amount, monthly spend, invoice status, and next renewal risk.
