SmartCloud Marketing Agent Guide - Poster, H5 Landing Page, and Product Promotion

Document type: marketing playbook
Source type: playbook
Trust label: SmartCloud support playbook

Marketing package
- Product summary: one short paragraph describing the product and target scenario.
- Selling points: 3 to 5 bullet points suitable for domestic cloud service promotion.
- Poster copy: short Chinese headline, subheadline, benefits, and call to action.
- H5 landing page: hero section, product value, scenario, pricing hint, lead form placeholder.
- Share link: public asset URL generated by the backend.

Example ECS promotion
- Headline: 企业上云第一台服务器，就选 SmartCloud ECS
- Selling points: 秒级开通、弹性扩容、安全组防护、监控告警、按量付费。
- CTA: 立即领取新用户云服务器试用包。

Example GPU promotion
- Headline: 大模型部署加速，GPU 算力开箱即用
- Selling points: 支持推理服务、向量检索、批量 embedding、稳定显存、成本可控。
- CTA: 获取 AI 算力方案评估。

Agent behavior
- Ops Marketing Agent should request product facts from Product Tech Agent through A2A before generating marketing content.
- Marketing output must avoid unsupported claims such as guaranteed revenue, unlimited free resources, or official certifications not present in the product catalog.
